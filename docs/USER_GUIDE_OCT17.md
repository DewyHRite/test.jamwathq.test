## ğŸ¯ Common Questions

### Q: Do I need to log in before clicking "Leave a Review"?
**A:** No! You can click "Leave a Review" first. If you're not logged in, we'll show you login options. After logging in, you'll automatically return to the same agency with the review form open.

### Q: Why do I need to log in at all?
**A:** We require authentication to ensure all reviews come from real J-1 participants. This maintains the authenticity and trustworthiness of the reviews on JamWatHQ.

### Q: What happens to my review form if I close the browser during login?
**A:** Your progress might be lost if you close the browser. We recommend completing the entire process in one session. However, if you return to the site after logging in, you can simply click "Leave a Review" again to start fresh.

### Q: Where is my review after I submit it?
**A:** Your review is immediately saved to the database and included in the agency's average rating. To see it displayed:
1. Click **"View Past Reviews"** on the same agency
2. Your review will appear at the top of the list (most recent first)
3. You'll see your Google profile picture (or your initial) next to your name

### Q: Can I see past reviews without leaving one?
**A:** Yes! Click **"View Past Reviews"** on any agency card to read reviews from other users. You don't need to submit your own review first.

### Q: Why are the buttons in a different order now?
**A:** We've reorganized the buttons to put the most common action first. Since more people want to leave reviews than view existing ones, **"Leave a Review"** is now the first button you'll see. This makes the interface more intuitive!

### Q: Will past reviews automatically appear after I submit mine?
**A:** No. After submitting your review, the card returns to its normal expanded view. This gives you a clean slate. If you want to see your review (and others), click **"View Past Reviews"** manually.

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Leave a Review" button doesn't respond
**Solution:**
1. Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
2. Try refreshing the page (Ctrl + R or Cmd + R)
3. Clear your browser cache (Ctrl + Shift + R or Cmd + Shift + R)

### Issue: Login modal doesn't appear when I click "Leave a Review"
**Solution:**
1. Check if you're already logged in (look for your name in the top right)
2. If logged in, the form should open directly (no login needed)
3. Try disabling ad blockers or popup blockers
4. Refresh the page and try again

### Issue: After logging in, I'm not returned to the agency
**Solution:**
1. This might happen if you opened the login in a new tab
2. Return to the original tab with the agencies page
3. Your session should be active - click "Leave a Review" again
4. The form should now open without asking you to log in again

### Issue: My review doesn't appear after submitting
**Solution:**
1. Check for a success message - if you saw it, your review was saved
2. Click **"View Past Reviews"** to see your review in the list
3. Look for your name and profile picture at the top
4. If it's not there, try refreshing the page
5. If still missing, please contact support

### Issue: Buttons are in the wrong order
**Solution:**
1. Clear your browser cache completely
2. Do a hard refresh (Ctrl + Shift + R or Cmd + Shift + R)
3. Try opening the page in incognito/private mode
4. If the issue persists, close and reopen your browser
